An iPhone wallpaper that I made with a computer. After many attempts, it became apparent that scratching the screen with a pencil wasn't a valid method for creating digital art, so I tried using the bar of soap thing that had thick string attaching it to the computer box. Anyway, the mountains were modeled in Blender, which is free (get it). Then using a combination of Photoshop and Flash, I added the sky and reflection. If you enjoy this wallpaper, remember to rate it 5 stars somehow. Just click on it really hard and I'll be notified eventually.

I was contacted through a connection of a connection of mine to design a logo for an eminently launching company, so after negotiating the terms of my contract I accepted the job. With a wry smile on my face and a devious twinkle in my eye, I reached back and removed the satchel resting on my shoulder. I slid it across the table, and before it could come to a stop, heaps of dough were poured in by my client's associates. All the while, I refused to break eye contact with the figure across from me who was almost completely silhouetted save for the weary, cautious white ellipses that served for eyes… Uh, anyway, my mom's friend asked me to make a logo, so I did.

His current icon was a generic, vaguely metallic play button over a circle. Woo. In fairness, this wouldn't really do much to set his app apart.
